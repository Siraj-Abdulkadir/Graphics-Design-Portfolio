Heaven & Co,

Role: UX/UI Designer & Prototyper

This project involves designing and prototyping a sophisticated website for Heaven & Co., a high-end interior design agency. The aim was to create a digital presence that reflects the agency’s ethos of elegance, harmony, and innovation in transforming spaces. The design emphasizes a seamless user experience and a visually striking aesthetic to captivate potential clients.

Cassian Frost Portfolio

Role: UX/UI Designer & Prototyper
For this project i recreate an award winning scroll animations and interactions. And redesign Cassian Frost´s Digital Portfolio,  an art director and photographer based in New York s known for his visually captivating and innovative web presence. The project focused on replicating the seamless scroll animations, engaging interactions, and overall aesthetic design. The most technically demanding aspect was achieving smooth and visually cohesive page transitions. However, through careful attention to detail and experimentation, I succesfully brought this concept
to life.

Ethan Caldwell Portfolio

Role: UX/UI Designer & Prototyper
This project focuses on designing and prototyping a modern, visually engaging portfolio for Ethan Caldwell, A photographer based in New York. The goal was to create a digital space that showcases his creative vision and photographic work while reflecting his unique artistic style and professional identity.

This portfolio reflects a balance of functionality and aesthetics, providing a platform that not only showcases Ethan’s photography but also enhances the storytelling of each project.

YSL - Men´s Spring ´25 Collection

Role: UX/UI Designer & Prototyper

I recently embarked on a conceptual redesign and prototype for YSL Men's Spring ’25 Collection. This project was a personal exploration with the sole purpose of experimenting, such as mastering visual hierarchy and crafting a user friendly experience. One of the biggest challenges was ensuring that the design didn’t stray from the elegance and timeless essence of the iconic YSL brand. I focused on blending creativity with functionality, aiming to capture the spirit of the collection while enhancing the overall user experience.

La Bella Tavola

Role: UX/UI Designer & Prototyper

This project presents a conceptual design for La Bella Tavola, an Italian restaurant that combines traditional flavors with a modern dining experience. The aim was to envision a visually appetizing digital platform that embodies the essence of authentic Italian cuisine while offering an intuitive and immersive user experience.
